Method — and the honest verdict

Win%: margin-aware Elo (K=20, log-margin multiplier, autocorrelation damping), home field ~48 Elo, rest-day differential, one-third preseason regression. 64.6% walk-forward over 16 seasons (4,350 games, Brier 0.2205).

The measured verdict: the closing market hits 66.4% on the same games. In the 693 games where model and market disagreed, the model was right 44.3% — fading the market with this model loses money, measured, not inferred. So the Edges tab sizes line-shopping only; model win% is never used as a betting signal.

Edges: per-book devig → median no-vig consensus → best available price → quarter-Kelly on the price gap, 2% bankroll cap, 3-book minimum.

Grading: every published probability is logged pre-game and settled against the final score; the Calibration tab is the model's public report card, including the live disagreement record.
